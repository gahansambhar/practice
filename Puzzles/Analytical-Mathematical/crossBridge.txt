Puzzle: There are 4 persons (A, B, C and D) who want to cross a bridge at night.

A takes 1 minute to cross the bridge.
B takes 2 minutes to cross the bridge.
C takes 5 minutes to cross the bridge.
D takes 8 minutes to cross the bridge.

There is only one torch with them and the bridge cannot be crossed without the torch. 
There cannot be more than two persons on the bridge at any time.

Solution:

A and B must cross together and A must come back: time taken 3 minutes

C and D should cross together and send B back: time taken 10 minutes

A and B must cross together: time taken 2 minutes

Everyone has crossed: time taken 15 minutes
