Question: 
You begin in the top left corner of a 6Ã—6 grid, and your objective is to move to the bottom right corner. 
There are just two directions you can move: right or down. Both diagonal and backward movements are prohibited. 
How many different ways are there to get from the start to the finish?

Answer:

Using combinatorics, this is simply a matter of chosing the positioning of 5 Downward moves out of 10 required moves. 

5C10 =  252 is the answer
